Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire is an epic action-drama set in the fictional, dystopian city-state of Khansaar, a world governed by its own brutal laws and warring tribes. The complex narrative centers on the deep, fraught bond between two childhood friends, Deva (Prabhas) and Vardharaja Mannar (Prithviraj Sukumaran), that ultimately turns them into formidable adversaries. The story unfolds when Vardharaja, the prince of Khansaar, faces a planned coup d'état by his father's ministers and relatives who all vie for control of the throne. To consolidate his power and survive the political turmoil, he leaves the city and seeks out his long-lost friend, Deva, who has been living a quiet life as a simple man for years. Bound by an old promise, Deva returns to Khansaar to help Vardharaja secure his position as the undisputed ruler, unleashing his incredibly violent and formidable nature in a series of high-octane battles. As the film progresses, it delves into the intricate history of Khansaar's various tribes, political conspiracies, and a major twist concerning Deva's true identity: he is actually Devaratha Raisaar, the son of the leader of the Shouryaanga tribe, the rightful heir to the throne, whose people were massacred years ago. The first part ends as Vardharaja, now aware of Deva's lineage and potential claim, declares him his "Salaar" (commander) while setting the stage for the inevitable conflict and power struggle in the announced sequel,
